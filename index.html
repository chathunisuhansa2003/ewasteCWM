Welcome to Ceylon Waste Management

Our Journey

Ceylon Waste Management Pvt Ltd was founded in 2010 as a Board of investment approved industry. CWM was penetrated to E- Waste industry while number of E- Waste trading companies were involving some aspects of E- Waste Recycling , mainly exporting . CWM penetrated to E- Waste Industry with sophisticated business Plan which is still continuing .

CWM was focused on Environmental compliances unlike competitors focused more on sales than compliances. Still , CWM robust regulatory and compliance related team than sales professionals in the company.
