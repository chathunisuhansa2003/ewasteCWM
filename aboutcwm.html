About CWM Group

Ceylon Waste Management (CWM) Ltd is the leading scientific e-waste management company in Sri Lanka with established industry benchmark for e-waste pre-processing and base metal and precious metal refining procedures in the region.

CWM Ltd is the leading scientific e-waste management company which established an industry benchmark for E-Waste Processing Indo-Pacific Economic Corridor. Secure data destruction services ensure zeroization of data leakage of corporate and personal devices.

CWM Ltd is aligned to comply with the growing demand of e-device consumers in the Indo-Pacific Economic region. Our operational strategies are focused on sustainability, environmental protection, data security and circular economy.

As an industry pioneer and market leader, CWM offers fully integrated e-waste management solutions for Sri Lanka, government institutions, business organizations and many other institutions.

E-Waste is a fast-growing waste stream in the world today. CWM has analyzed the importance of “Data Security” in a digitalized economy where e-commerce strategies, social media and handheld devices are in operation. CWM ensures the prevention of “Data Leakage” which might cause a huge financial reputation loss for a business organization or individual. CWM has designed a novel data destruction methodology that “Melt data at 1300 0C “temperature inside an industrial furnace & align with circular economy by extracting precious metal.
