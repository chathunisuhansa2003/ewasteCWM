Vision & Mission

Our Vision and the Mission has been strategically Connected by inherent Core Values of the company to deliver best service to our Customers, Suppliers, Employees, People, Society, and the Environment. CWM Vision, Mission and core values aligned to the achievement of Triple Bottom Line Concept which is People, Planet, and the Profit.

Vision

To strategically align waste management procedures to become the world’s most secure company for personal & corporate security data destruction and e-waste management.

Mission

Is to establish strategic partnerships with brands, corporates, suppliers and recyclers in the world, in value-sharing basis.

Core Values

  • Environmental accountability
  • Commitment
  • Downstream process transparency
  • Sharing Profits with all stakeholders
  • Reliability
